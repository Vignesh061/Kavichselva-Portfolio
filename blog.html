Iron Condor Strategy: Maximizing Premium Decay in NIFTY Weeklies

Learn how to set up and manage a successful Iron Condor strategy on NIFTY weekly options, one of the most popular non-directional strategies for consistent income.

Strategy Iron Condor
Risk Defined
Type Non-Directional

What is an Iron Condor?

An Iron Condor is a four-legged options strategy that involves selling an OTM call spread and an OTM put spread simultaneously. It profits when the underlying stays within a range. The maximum profit is the net premium received.

Setup Example (NIFTY at 22,400)

  • Sell 22,600 CE: Premium received: ₹45
  • Buy 22,700 CE: Premium paid: ₹20
  • Sell 22,200 PE: Premium received: ₹40
  • Buy 22,100 PE: Premium paid: ₹18
  • Net Credit: ₹47 per lot (₹2,350)
  • Max Risk: ₹53 per lot (₹2,650)

When to Use

Iron Condors work best in low-volatility environments when India VIX is below 14. Avoid this strategy before major events like RBI policy, budget, or quarterly results.

Mastering Candlestick Patterns: The Complete Beginner's Guide

Candlestick patterns are the foundation of technical analysis. Learn how to read and interpret the most powerful candlestick patterns used by professional traders worldwide.

Level Beginner
Patterns 15+
Type Educational

Single Candlestick Patterns

  • Hammer: Bullish reversal pattern found at the bottom of a downtrend. Long lower shadow with small body.
  • Shooting Star: Bearish reversal at the top. Long upper shadow with small body near the low.
  • Doji: Indecision candle where open ≈ close. Watch for confirmation in the next candle.
  • Marubozu: Strong momentum candle with no shadows. Indicates strong buying or selling pressure.

Double Candlestick Patterns

  • Bullish Engulfing: A large green candle completely engulfs the previous red candle. Strong buy signal.
  • Bearish Engulfing: Opposite of bullish engulfing. Strong sell signal at market tops.
  • Piercing Line: Green candle opens below previous close and closes above 50% of the red candle.

Bank NIFTY Expiry Day Strategy: Quick Scalping Setup

A proven Bank NIFTY expiry day trading strategy using price action and option chain data. This setup has shown consistent results with proper risk management.

Index Bank NIFTY
Win Rate 72%
Style Scalping

Strategy Rules

  • Wait for the first 15-minute candle to close after market opens
  • Identify the high and low of this candle as the range
  • Go long (buy CE) if price breaks above the high with volume
  • Go short (buy PE) if price breaks below the low with volume
  • Stop loss: Opposite side of the 15-min range
  • Target: 1.5x to 2x of risk

⚠️ Risk Management

Never risk more than 2% of your capital on a single trade. On expiry days, theta decay is extremely rapid. Only trade with money you can afford to lose. This strategy requires discipline and quick execution.
